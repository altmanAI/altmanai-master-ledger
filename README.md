## Evidence standard

The Master Ledger is not a claim that every AltmanAI system is finished, independently certified, or production-ready. Records must distinguish:

- **established** from proposed;
- **working** from planned;
- **tested** from assumed;
- **verified** from self-reported; and
- **current records** from historical artifacts.

Signed, sealed, minted, or hash-bound artifacts are not silently rewritten. Corrections use an inspectable amendment or versioned successor.

## Contributing a record

Open a focused issue or pull request and include:

1. the authorized objective;
2. accurate status and limitations;
3. evidence or artifact links;
4. a reproducible hash when applicable;
5. human and AI contribution attribution;
6. security and privacy review; and
7. the required Founder approval record for authoritative changes.
